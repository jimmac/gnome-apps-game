What is this?

A retro pixel-art quiz game featuring 166 GNOME app icons rendered as 32×32 sprites. Browse the collection, guess the app, then reveal the answer. Built with LÖVE and designed to run on everything from handhelds to desktops.

🎮

Handheld Ready

Runs natively on the Anbernic RG CubeXX via KNULLI and PortMaster.

📺

CRT Effects

Authentic scanlines, barrel distortion, and glitch effects. Toggle with Tab.

🖼️

166 Icons

Hand-crafted pixel art of GNOME and Flathub app icons at 32×32.

⌨️

Keyboard & Gamepad

Full support for keyboard, D-pad, and gamepad controls.

🌙

Screensaver Mode

Idle for 30 seconds and a scrolling icon mosaic takes over with the Flathub logo.

🔧

Open Source

MIT licensed. Built with LÖVE 11.5 and plain Lua.
