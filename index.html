What is this?

A retro pixel-art quiz game featuring dozens of GNOME apps. Browse the collection, guess the app, then reveal the answer. Built with LÖVE and designed to run on everything from handhelds to major desktop OS.

Handheld Ready

Runs on handhelds with KNULLI or ROCKNIX firmware and PortMaster. Also works on Steam Deck via Flatpak.

Pixel Art Galore

Every app icon is a hand-crafted pixel art piece with its own reveal animation. Enjoy the detail.

Screensaver Mode

Idle and let the apps flow — a mesmerizing parade of icons cascades before you.
