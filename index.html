What is this?

A retro pixel-art quiz game featuring dozens of GNOME apps. Browse the collection, guess the app, then reveal the answer. Built with LÖVE and designed to run on everything from handhelds to major desktop OS.

Handheld Ready

Runs on Anbernic, Powkiddy, Miyoo, Retroid and other handhelds via PortMaster. Works on Steam Deck too.

Keyboard & Gamepad

Full support for keyboard, D-pad, and gamepad controls.

Screensaver Mode

Idle for 30 seconds and a scrolling icon mosaic takes over with the Flathub logo.
